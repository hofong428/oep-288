# OEP-288 (Public-Minimal Draft)

**Goal:** A cloud-neutral receipt proving *what* ran and *what* it produced — without
storing PHI/PII.

## Minimal Receipt (fields)
- `id` (UUID v7)
- `ts` (ISO-8601 UTC)
- `subject.type` (`model_inference` | `sbom_generation` | `data_processing`)
- `subject.name` (string)
- `input_hash`, `output_hash` (hex SHA-256)
- `env.cloud` (`aws` | `azure` | `gcp` | `on-prem` | `edge`)
- `env.region` (string)
- `attest_ref.type` (`tdx` | `sev` | `nitro` | `sgx` | `tpm` | `none`) — optional
- `merkle.root` (hex)
- `transparency.rekor_entry_uuid` (hex)
- `signatures[]` (placeholder signer ids + base64 signatures)

**All endpoints and key ids are placeholders, e.g.:**
- Rekor URL example: `https://rekor.example.invalid`
- KMS URI example: `aws-kms://example-region-1111:key/example-uuid`

> This document intentionally omits vendor-specific details and crypto routines.
